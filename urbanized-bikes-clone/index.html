Built for the ultimate carefree city ride

No hassle. No flat tyres. No parts to replace.

Urbanized is an award winning designer bicycle, engineered for the ultimate urban comfort and ease of use. Inspired by city living, the URBANIZED bicycle comes packed full of thoughtful features for everyday use to seamlessly compliment your lifestyle. It is maintenance free, weatherproof and portable.

Maintenance free and weatherproof

All Urbanized bikes feature the stronger and quieter, grease free belt drive system instead of a chain, and solid tires that never go flat. The timeproof roller brakes and internal gear hub complete the list of the ultimate low maintenance and weatherproof components.
+ Learn More

Built with the city in mind

Experience the city-friendly functionality. Change gears while waiting at the traffic light and be the first to kick off. Use the specially designed handle to carry the bicycle effortlessly up and down steps without the fear that the front wheel can turn back and soil your clothes. There is a stabilizer to hold it straight. Feel confident in your breaks even in bad weather conditions.

Award winning design to suit you

Our designer bicycles are among the winners of one of the world’s largest design competitions: The Red Dot Award! Urbanized maintenance free commuting bicycle is also Winner in Vehicle, Mobility and Transportation Design Category 2017-2018 . Whether it is the daily commute to work or the cycle ride to your favourite café, the URBANIZED bicycle proves that style and comfort are not mutually exclusive. Our comprehensive range of seasonal colors is available for all models and sizes.
